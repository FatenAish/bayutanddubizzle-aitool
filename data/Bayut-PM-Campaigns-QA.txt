Bayut PM Campaigns – Q&A

Q: What are PM Campaigns at Bayut?
A: PM Campaigns are paid marketing campaigns planned and executed to drive traffic and awareness for Bayut content and priority pages, while aligning with budget and business goals.

Q: What is the purpose of PM Campaigns?
A: The purpose is to standardize how paid campaigns are planned, produced, launched, and tracked across Content, Design, and Performance teams.

Q: Which teams are involved in PM Campaigns?
A: PM Campaigns involve the Content Team, Design Team, and Performance Marketing Team.

Q: What types of campaigns are covered?
A:
- Traffic campaigns (FB Display + SEM DSA for Market Trends)
- Image Ads and Carousel Ads
- Budget assignment, tracking, and reporting

Q: How often are PM Campaigns launched?
A: PM Campaigns are launched monthly, depending on budget availability.

Q: Who is responsible for PM Campaigns?
A:
- Content Marketing Team: suggests topics, writes ad copy, manages workflow, and approvals
- Design Team: creates campaign creatives
- Performance Marketing Team: launches campaigns, sets targeting and budgets, and shares results

Q: What are the main objectives of PM Campaigns?
A:
- Traffic: drive sessions to content and priority pages
- Awareness: increase brand visibility for new projects or services

Q: Which channels and formats are allowed?
A:
- FB Display: Image Ads and Carousel Ads
- SEM – DSA (Market Trends): feed-based ads promoting market trends articles

Q: How is the PM budget managed?
A:
- The annual PM budget is confirmed by Performance Marketing
- The full yearly budget must be consumed within the year
- Budget and campaign data are tracked in the “PM Content Campaigns” sheet

Q: How do PM Campaigns work step by step?
A:
1. Performance Marketing confirms the budget for the cycle
2. Content Team sets objectives and selects channels
3. Content team prepares ad copy and updates the campaign sheet
4. Content is reviewed and approved
5. Design team creates creatives via Airtable
6. Ads are sent for marketing approval
7. Content team requests launch from Performance Marketing via Slack
8. Performance Marketing launches the campaigns
9. Results are shared monthly and documented
10. Year-end review ensures budget consumption and archives assets
