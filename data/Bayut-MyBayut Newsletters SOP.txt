MyBayut 
Newsletters SOP
  

This SOP outlines the end-to-end process for planning, creating, reviewing, and sending out the MyBayut B2C weekly newsletters. These newsletters serve as a key communication channel between the brand and its audience, highlighting engaging and informative blog articles that reflect MyBayut’s voice and values.
The SOP ensures that the newsletter creation process is consistent, timely, and aligned with MyBayut’s editorial and marketing objectives.
Purpose
The purpose of this SOP is to define a clear and structured process for producing and delivering weekly newsletters that feature content exclusively from the MyBayut blog. These newsletters aim to:
* Deliver valuable and relevant blog content to readers in an engaging format.
* Increase user engagement and encourage recurring visits to the MyBayut blog.
* Drive qualified traffic to the website through targeted, high-quality articles.
* Maintain consistency in editorial tone, visual identity, and publishing timelines.
* Enhance brand presence by positioning MyBayut as a trusted real estate and lifestyle resource.
Responsibilities:
* Content Marketing Team:
   * Prepares the monthly content calendar for the AR and EN B2C newsletters.
   * Selects relevant topics and articles to be featured.
   * Writes customised content tailored to the audience and brand tone.
   * Conducts internal testing to ensure accuracy in links, layout, and visuals.
   * Monitors performance to ensure campaigns achieve optimal results.
* Performance Marketing Team:
   * Handles the technical setup and implementation of the newsletter

Step-by-Step Workflow
Step 1: Monthly Content Calendar
* Prepare the monthly newsletter calendar by the 14th of every month
* The calendar must include newsletter topics, featured articles, and links
* Submit your work for review and approval by Sarah before proceeding to content creation
Step 2: Content Creation
* Prepare newsletter content weekly based on the approved calendar
Step 3: Visuals/Design Requests
* Submit visuals request to the Design Team after the monthly calendar is approved
* Images must follow Bayut branding guidelines and match the newsletter layout
* The writer is responsible for ensuring that all required visuals are submitted on time
Step 4: Content Submission on Airtable
* Upload the final newsletter copy, media, and links to the Airtable – Newsletter Section
* Update the Status to "Ready for Editing"
* The newsletter is automatically assigned to editors for review
Step 5: Language Review & Status Update
* The assigned Content Leads review content quality:
   * Arabic newsletters: reviewed by Fatina AlHalaiqa
   * English newsletters: reviewed by Sabeeh Rizvi
* After edits and approval, the Editors change the Status in Airtable to "Ready for Testing"
Step 6: Internal Testing
* Once marked as "Ready for Testing", the newsletter is shared automatically with the team via the #bayut-newsletter Slack group
* A test send is triggered on MoEngage for internal review
* All stakeholders (writers, editors, and POCs) must review:
   * Layout & formatting
   * Links and buttons functionality
   * Visual display and mobile view
   * Correct blog/article links
Step 7: Final Approval & Sending
* After the test version is approved by the content team, it is submitted to Sarah Alnawahfor final approval
* After approval, the Performance Marketing Team schedules the newsletter for distribution to the audience.
