Product Content POC  
The purpose of this SOP is to define the role, responsibilities, and workflows for the Product Point of Contact (POC) for the Arabic Content Team.
Purpose
This process ensures effective communication between the Product and Arabic Content teams, facilitates smooth implementation of new features, and supports product-related operations for Arabic content across platforms.
Responsibilities
* Act as the main communication bridge between the Product and Arabic Content teams
* Discuss the details of new features with the Product team before and after launch
* Test new features post-launch to ensure proper functionality and support for Arabic content workflows
* Share product updates, launches, and enhancements with the Arabic Content team promptly
* Support the team with Strapi technical issues and queries
* Prepare and share product monitoring reports that highlight key updates, system performance, and areas for improvement
* Collaborate with the Product team to ensure product features are optimised for Arabic content

Step-by-Step Workflow
Step 1: Features Discussion and Planning
Coordinate with the Product team, in meetings or via group discussions, to review upcoming features, objectives, and their impact on content workflows.
Step 2: Features Testing and Validation
* Test all new features post-launch to verify overall functionality
* Confirm that all text elements, buttons, and system messages display correctly across layouts
* Verify the accuracy of Arabic translations in all feature-related texts
* Report findings and raise any detected issues to the Product team for resolution
* Conduct follow-up testing after fixes or updates to confirm full resolution
Step 3: Product Updates Communication
* Act as the first point of contact for team queries regarding product functionality and Strapi usage
* Provide direct support to the Arabic team for any technical or workflow issues
* Receive and review all product-related announcements from the Product team
* Share detailed updates on product updates, bug fixes, or new features with the Arabic team
* Keep a record of all new features, updates, and feedback for future reference
Frequency 
* Feature review and planning: Bi-weekly or per release cycle
* Testing and validation: After each feature launch
* Team support: Ongoing, as needed
* Product updates communication: As updates, launches, or enhancements occur.
Monitoring & Performance Analysis
* Track system performance and product impact on content operations using Looker Studio dashboards
* Collect feedback from the Arabic team to identify recurring issues or improvement areas
* Prepare periodic reports highlighting key updates, system performance, and suggested optimizations
