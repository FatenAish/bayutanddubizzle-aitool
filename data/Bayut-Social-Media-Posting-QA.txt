Q: How often is the social media calendar prepared?
A: A monthly content calendar is prepared before the 10th of each month for the upcoming month and shared with the Social Media team after approval. fileciteturn5file0

Q: What does the social media calendar include?
A:
- Month
- Theme or campaign
- Language
- Priority level
- Post details and link
- Captions (if required)
The calendar includes up to 20 post ideas, from which the Social Media team selects based on their publishing schedule. fileciteturn5file0

Q: How does the social media content planning process work?
A:
1. Content Team fills in the Social Media Calendar sheet with all required details before the 10th
2. Social Media Team reviews and selects posts based on scheduling needs fileciteturn5file0

Q: How are creative requests handled?
A:
1. Social Media Team prepares a creative brief including title, caption, visual concept, and platform dimensions
2. Approval is obtained from Areej Tawfiq Abu Reida and Sarah Alnawah
3. The approved brief is uploaded to the Design Request Form
4. Designers are given access to start creative development fileciteturn5file0

Q: How are creatives approved and published?
A:
1. Design Team secures final design approval
2. Content Team uploads approved creatives to Airtable
3. Social Media Team schedules or publishes the post on Instagram fileciteturn5file0
